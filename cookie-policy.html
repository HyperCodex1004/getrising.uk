Cookie Policy

Updated on 20.11.2024

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or smartphone) when you visit a website. They help websites recognize your device, enhance your browsing experience, and track certain information for analytics or functionality purposes.


2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Provide essential website functionality (e.g., making the contact form work properly).
  • Understand website traffic and visitor behavior through Google Analytics to improve our website’s performance and user experience.

The cookies used on www.getrising.uk fall into the following categories:

a. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. Without these cookies, certain features (like the contact form) may not work.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
XSRF-TOKEN Protects the website from cross-site scripting (XSS) attacks. Session

b. Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to collect information about how visitors use our website. This helps us analyze data such as visitor numbers, page popularity, and traffic sources. Google Analytics cookies collect information anonymously.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
_ga Distinguishes unique users. 2 years
_gid Tracks user behavior across sessions. 24 hours
_gat Throttles request rates to Google Analytics. 1 minute

For more information, see Google’s Privacy Policy and learn how to opt-out of Google Analytics.


3. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in the following ways:

a. Consent

Upon your first visit to www.getrising.uk, you will see a cookie banner requesting your consent for analytics cookies. Essential cookies will always be used as they are necessary for website functionality.

b. Browser Settings

You can block or delete cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may impact the functionality of our website. Below are links to manage cookies in common browsers:

  • Manage cookies in Chrome
  • Manage cookies in Firefox
  • Manage cookies in Edge

4. Third-Party Cookies

Our website uses cookies provided by Google Analytics, a third-party service. These cookies collect anonymous data about your website usage and interactions.


5. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our use of cookies or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated effective date.


6. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Cookie Policy or how we use cookies, please contact us.
