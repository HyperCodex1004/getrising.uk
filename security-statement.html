Security Statement

Updated on 02.04.2025

At Rising Ltd, we take the security of your information seriously. As a company specializing in cybersecurity strategy, we are committed to safeguarding the data and privacy of our clients, partners, and website visitors.


Our Security Principles

  • Confidentiality: We only collect and store personal data when necessary, and restrict access based on need and role.
  • Integrity: We maintain the accuracy and completeness of data through reliable systems and monitoring practices.
  • Availability: Our services are designed to be resilient, secure, and reliably available when needed.

Website Security

Our website uses SSL/TLS encryption to secure the transmission of data. You can verify this by checking the padlock icon in your browser’s address bar.

We regularly update our website, CMS, plugins, and dependencies to address known vulnerabilities and implement industry best practices.


Data Protection

We adhere to GDPR and the UK Data Protection Act 2018. Any personal data collected through contact forms or cookies is handled in accordance with our Privacy Policy.


Hosting and Infrastructure

Our site is hosted by reputable third-party providers who implement strong physical and network security measures. We choose vendors that comply with ISO 27001 or equivalent standards whenever possible.


Responsible Disclosure

If you believe you have found a security vulnerability on our website or systems, we encourage responsible disclosure. Please contact us immediately. We will investigate promptly and take appropriate action.


Contact

For questions related to data, information security, or anything else please contact us.
